Every Vesta professional undergoes a comprehensive screening process that includes: national criminal background check, county-level records search, sex offender registry check, reference verification with previous employers or clients, and for childcare professionals, an additional police verification step. The entire process typically takes 3–5 business days. No professional is activated on the platform until all checks are complete and cleared.
When you book a service, Vesta generates a unique 4-digit code (e.g., VES-4872) tied to your booking. After the professional completes your service, they ask you for this code. When you provide it, payment is automatically released to the professional. This means you are always in control — if you're not satisfied, simply don't share the code, and our support team will resolve the situation. It's our way of ensuring every payment is earned and every customer is happy.
For emergency same-day bookings, our AI matching engine guarantees a verified professional within 15 minutes. For scheduled bookings, you can select any available time slot — as early as tomorrow. The 15-minute guarantee applies 7 days a week, from 7am to 10pm PT.
Vesta currently serves San Francisco and the Peninsula (including Palo Alto, Mountain View, San Jose, and surrounding cities). In Phase 3 (2026), we'll expand to Marin, East Bay, and Napa/Sonoma. Enter your ZIP code during booking to confirm availability in your area.
Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the scheduled service are fully refunded with no fee. Cancellations within 24 hours incur a 25% cancellation fee. If a professional cancels, you are fully refunded and we prioritize rematching you immediately. If you're unsatisfied with a completed service, we'll either re-do the service at no charge or issue a full refund — no questions asked.
Click "Join as a Pro" anywhere on the site to submit your application. We'll need your name, contact information, service type, and consent to a background check. Applications are reviewed within 48 hours, and the background check process takes 3–5 business days. Once cleared, you'll complete a brief virtual onboarding session and receive access to the professional dashboard.
New professionals start at a 20–25% commission rate. As you build your rating and booking history on the platform, you unlock lower rates: 18% after 50 bookings with a 4.7+ rating, 15% after 150 bookings with a 4.8+ rating. There are no other fees — no listing fees, no subscription, no equipment rental. You keep everything above the commission.
