Why Vesta exists.

The home services industry is fundamentally broken. TaskRabbit and Thumbtack are glorified classifieds — they hand you a stranger's phone number and call it a service. Handy covers two categories. Care.com hides professionals behind a subscription wall.

Meanwhile, the professionals doing this work — cleaners, nannies, chefs, laundry specialists — are treated as commodities. They hustle for leads, get paid inconsistently, and receive none of the benefits that come with steady employment.

Vesta was created to fix both sides of this equation. Customers get a white-glove, zero-friction experience with verified professionals and fixed prices. Professionals get a steady income stream, dignified work, and benefits access. Everyone wins.

Our Values

What we stand for.

These aren't aspirational words on a wall — they're the operating principles that shape every product decision, every partnership, and every hire we make.

01
🛡️
Trust Above All
Every professional on Vesta undergoes background checks, police verification, sex offender registry screening, and reference confirmation before their first booking. Trust is not a feature — it's the foundation of everything we build. We will never cut corners on safety, no matter the speed pressure or cost.
02
🔍
Radical Transparency
Fixed pricing. No hidden fees. No bidding wars. No surprise charges. Professionals see exactly what they earn and what commission they pay. Customers see exactly what they pay — before they book. Transparency isn't just honest; it's good business. It builds the kind of trust that makes people come back.
03
🤝
Professional Dignity
The people who clean homes, care for children, and prepare meals are professionals — not gig workers to be exploited. Vesta pays fairly, pays on time, and provides tools that help professionals grow their income and build their reputation. Benefits access, performance-based commission reductions, and career development are core to what we offer.
04
⚡
Operational Excellence
A 15-minute match guarantee is a bold promise. Keeping it requires outstanding engineering, deep supply development, and relentless process optimization. We don't ship features — we ship experiences. Every booking should feel effortless, every interaction should feel considered, every outcome should meet the gold standard we've set for ourselves.
05
🌱
Long-Term Thinking
We're building a company that will last decades, not a feature to flip in 18 months. That means investing in professional relationships, building a brand that homeowners recommend to their neighbors, and expanding deliberately — Bay Area first, then nationwide. Speed matters, but sustainable speed matters more.
